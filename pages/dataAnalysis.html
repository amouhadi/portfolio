City Management System

Dolphin Apadana

Dolphin Apadana company provides intelligent fleet management solutions and cooperate with two city halls to improve city management system. This project focuses on developing a framework for city hall employee to record and analyze construction penalty of buildings.

In this project, we preprocessed, cleaned and sampled the construction data from Excel or XML file for data analysis and then use prediction method to predict construction penalty. We also developed the front-end framework for municipal employee to access these records.
